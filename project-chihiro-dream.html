Chihiro's Dream

Graphic Design

Made in 2024
Art Nouveau Style

Design Intent

Our design intent is to display Chihiro's journey with the different characters in the film and how each of them impacted her through the use of rhythmic obstacles and contrasting angles.

Chihiro will exhibit a venturesome growth from being timid to someone who does not flinch when faced with peculiar experiences in the spiritual realm.

Done By: Lim Sin Xuan & Daphne
