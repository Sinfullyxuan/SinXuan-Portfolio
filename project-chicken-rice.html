Chicken Rice

Graphic Design

Made in 2024
Art Nouveau Style

The nationally famous chicken rice is depicted in an Art Nouveau style through its ingredients. Using the main ingredient of the chicken as the focal point, I twisted and stretched its wings to create a more dynamic effect. I also used chili to represent the chicken’s comb. To frame the drawing, I elongated and curled the rice plant and coriander. Additionally, I deformed the ginger to resemble the chicken’s feet. For the background, I created an abstract representation of water droplets to symbolize the water used in cooking the dish.
